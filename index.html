Why it matters — even for a one-person business

Three ways everyday AI use can cost you.

You don't need to build AI to be at risk. Using it the wrong way is enough.

Data leak

Your data leaves the building

Free AI tools can store or train on what you paste. A client's details in a prompt can become a real data breach.

Illegal

It can break the law

GDPR — and Ukraine's incoming data law — require a legal basis, a record and a vendor agreement to process personal data. AI tools are no exception.

Wrong output

It can simply be wrong

AI invents and misreads facts with full confidence. Act on a wrong answer and the liability is yours, not the tool's.
